Headline Findings — September 2025

🔍 Critical Insights — Act on These

The Sep 1 LinkedIn document hit 56.1% CTR and 57.3% ER — the highest click-through rate of the entire 10-month dataset. "Building the future of startups, one expert at a time" was a community showcase document. This format — advisor/expert spotlights with a clear CTA — consistently breaks 30%+ CTR across every month it appears.
The Sep 25 Instagram reel — "Behind every milestone are the people who make it possible 🫶 Our COO..." — generated 66 shares and 22.52% ER, the highest IG share count of the entire dataset by an enormous margin. All other months combined average 5–10 shares. Authentic internal/people content has outsized viral potential on Instagram.
September's 17.6% avg LinkedIn ER is strong despite fewer posts (17). The Sep 1 post alone had 57.3% ER. Without it, the monthly avg would be ~12%. The data suggests fewer, higher-quality posts can match or exceed the performance of high-volume months — September proves this with 36,994 impressions from just 17 posts vs November's 64,302 from 24 posts.
+1,847 new LinkedIn followers in September — second only to October's 2,147. The Sep 15 peak of 267 in a single day was likely driven by an event or announcement (New York events often had this effect). September was consistently a high-growth month.
Instagram's 8 posts had a striking bimodal distribution: 7 posts averaged 3.2% ER, while the COO reel averaged 22.52% — a 7× gap. Internal people content dramatically outperforms branded/advisory content on Instagram. One authentic team moment drove more engagement than seven regular posts combined.
