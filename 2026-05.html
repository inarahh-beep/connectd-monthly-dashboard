🔍 Cross-Platform Audience Insight
Your Instagram audience is 64.6% male and concentrated in the 25–44 bracket (71.8% of followers) — professionals still building their careers. Your LinkedIn audience skews decisively more senior: Senior, Director, CXO, and VP levels together represent over 56% of followers.

You're reaching the same professional world at two different career stages. Instagram catches them when they're growing; LinkedIn reaches them when they're deciding. Content strategy should reflect this split: Instagram = career growth, aspiration, and relatable moments; LinkedIn = peer-level expertise, proof, and ROI.

Both audiences are heavily UK-based (Instagram 60.6% UK, LinkedIn ~30% London area), with India as a consistent #2 on both platforms — a potential growth market worth considering.
