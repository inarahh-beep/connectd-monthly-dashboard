Section 0
Headline Findings — December 2025

🔍 Critical Insights — Act on These

December was the best Instagram views month of the entire dataset — 15,551 total views across 8 posts. The QPR FC reel alone reached 1,194 unique accounts with 9 shares. Year-end and milestone content clearly resonates on Instagram in a way day-to-day content doesn't.
The Dec 31 year-end carousel was the standout post of December across both platforms: 37.7% CTR and 2,130 LinkedIn clicks; 7.93% IG ER with 5 saves and 19 likes. End-of-year wrap content performs consistently above average — this format should be planned and replicated at every major milestone.
The Telegraph article (Dec 5, Michael Horsley) reached 10,257 organic LinkedIn impressions — the second-highest organic reach post in the dataset — but only 4.0% CTR. It was also boosted with a sponsored campaign reaching 190K impressions. Press coverage drives reach but not clicks. Pair future press posts with a stronger CTA to convert the extra eyeballs.
Two major partnerships announced in December — QPR FC (Official Fractional Work Partner, Dec 18) and SyndicateRoom (Dec 16). Both were video posts: QPR drove 5,976 impressions and 16 reposts; SyndicateRoom drove 6.9% ER on LinkedIn. Partnership videos get reshared but don't drive clicks — they build brand.
Instagram carousels consistently outperformed reels in December: avg ER 3.88% (5 carousels) vs 2.77% (2 reels). The Dec 24 Christmas carousel (4.27% ER) and Dec 31 year-end carousel (7.93% ER) both beat every reel. The carousel format drives saves — 8 total IG saves in December, the joint-best with March.
