Section 0
Headline Findings — March 2026

🔍 Critical Insights — Act on These

March was Connectd's most active month — 23 LinkedIn posts + 11 Instagram posts = 34 total pieces of content. Despite the volume, avg LinkedIn ER held at 15.2% and avg Instagram ER at 5.63%, both strong for the frequency.
Two major press announcements in one month: FT 1000 Europe's Fastest-Growing Companies (#67, Mar 3) and Sifted 100 (Mar 18). The FT post drove 94 likes and 14 reposts — the most reshared single post in March — but only 2.0% CTR, suggesting press content gets shared but not clicked.
The Mar 31 Document "Most advisory roles don't come from a job description" hit 50.1% CTR — matching April's record. This is the second time this exact content format (advisor spotlight with a CTA to book a call) has crossed 50% CTR.
March had a US event push (Tampa Mar 17, Miami Mar 19, Dallas Mar 10, NYC recap Mar 19). The Florida events drove 4 LinkedIn posts, generating strong follower spikes — 149 new followers on Mar 26 alone, the highest single-day count this quarter.
Instagram performed consistently better in March than subsequent months: 11 posts, avg ER 5.63%, 5 saves, 9 shares. The Sifted 100 carousel (13.04% ER) and Times Square carousel (10.93%) show that milestone/event content drives the highest Instagram engagement.
