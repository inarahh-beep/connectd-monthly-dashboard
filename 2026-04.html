Headline Findings — April 2026

🔍 Critical Insights — Act on These

The Apr 21 Document post hit a 50.8% CTR — the highest recorded so far. "Everyone's talking about fractional work. Almost no one is describing it accurately." drove 1,412 clicks from 2,782 impressions. This is your content formula: direct challenge to a common misconception, followed by a document reveal.
⚠️ The Apr 17 sponsored boost underperformed vs organic. The giveaway post had 12.1% CTR organically but only 2.4% CTR from the sponsored promotion (22,507 sponsored impressions, 548 sponsored clicks). Organic outperformed paid 5× on CTR. The budget was likely spent reaching cold audiences who didn't click.
Document posts averaged 28.7% CTR in April — above the 26.4% dataset average. 6 of 12 LinkedIn posts were Documents. The format continues to dominate: Apr 14 (28.8%), Apr 15 (30.5%), Apr 28 (28.5%) all outperformed industry average by 8–15×.
Instagram saved 3 times in April — matching and slightly exceeding May's 2 saves. Apr 15, Apr 16, and Apr 17 carousels each earned 1 save. Carousels continue to be the format most likely to be bookmarked.
Only 917 new LinkedIn followers in April vs 1,593 in May. The May spike was driven by the CEO's personal job post (+313 in one day). Without a viral personal post, organic follower growth settles around 900–1,000/month.

🌱 Trend Gaps — Opportunities for Next Month
Based on dataset patterns and niche benchmarks. Updated each month during dashboard build.
📱
Carousels are the highest-ER Instagram format (avg 6–8% ER) but consistently underused.
Action: Repurpose LinkedIn advisor spotlight documents as Instagram carousels. Target 2+ carousels per month.
🎯
Document posts with CTAs average 20–30% CTR vs 3–5% for videos — the format gap is consistent across all months.
Action: At least one document post per week. Direct challenge hooks ("Most people get X wrong") are the highest-performing format.
🏷️
#FractionalCXO, #PortfolioCareer, #StartupAdvisor — trending niche hashtags rarely used.
Action: Add a 3-hashtag stack to every LinkedIn post. Extends reach to non-followers at zero effort.
🔁
Public LinkedIn mentions receive 0 reshares from the company page — free social proof left on the table every month.
Action: 5-minute weekly habit — check and reshare or comment on company mentions. See Section 9 for this month's confirmed mentions.
🎙️
Authentic people/team content massively outperforms branded content on Instagram.
Action: One team/founder/member short video per month. Record on iPhone, post natively. Sep 25 COO reel hit 22.52% ER — 7× the monthly average.
